##  Project Description

This repository contains my Digital Literacy project created as part of my academic coursework. The objective of this project was to understand the importance of digital awareness, professional online presence, cyber safety, and effective communication in the modern digital environment.

During this project, I explored different digital platforms, created professional profiles, practiced coding basics, learned email etiquette, and studied cybercrime awareness. This project helped me improve both my technical knowledge and my understanding of responsible digital behaviour.


##Project Objectives

Understand the concept of digital literacy  
 Build a professional digital portfolio  
Explore coding practice platforms  
Learn professional email communication  
Understand cybercrime and prevention methods  


## Project Structure

##Tasks Completed

###  Task 1 – Digital Literacy Infographic
I created an awareness infographic explaining digital literacy, safe internet usage, and useful digital tools using Canva. This task helped me understand how information can be presented visually for better understanding.

###  Task 2 – Digital Portfolio Creation
I created my accounts on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle to start building my professional presence. This helped me understand how these platforms will support my learning journey and career development.

### Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms
I solved a beginner coding problem on HackerRank and created a Google Form quiz related to digital literacy. This activity improved my problem-solving skills and introduced me to online collaboration tools.

### Task 4 – Professional Email Writing
I drafted formal emails and prepared a social media etiquette checklist. This task taught me how to communicate professionally and avoid common mistakes in digital communication.

### Task 5 – Cybercrime Case Study
I studied phishing cybercrime and prepared a prevention checklist. This helped me understand how cyber fraud happens and how students can stay safe online.
